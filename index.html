Frequently Asked Questions

How do I export my family tree?

You can export your tree as GEDCOM, PDF, or image from the Share menu inside the app.

Is my family data private?

Yes. Your trees are stored privately on your device and only uploaded if you choose to share them.

I’m having trouble adding relatives. What should I do?

Try clearing the app cache or reinstalling. If the issue continues, send us a screenshot via the form above.
